IPL Schedule 2025

IPL Schedule 2025: Today in the Indian Premier League (IPL), Delhi Capitals will host Lucknow Super Giants at the Dr. Y.S. Rajasekhara Reddy ACA-VDCA Cricket Stadium in Visakhapatnam. A day prior, on Sunday (March 23), In the first double-header of the season, Sunrisers Hyderabad and Chennai Super Kings posted wins against Rajasthan Royals and Mumbai Indians, respectively.

With the IPL 2025 underway, beginning on March 22 and scheduled to go until May 25, franchises announced their retained players, went to the table for their auction picks and for multiple franchises, new captaincy announcements have been made.

The season got underway at the Eden Gardens with defending champions Kolkata Knight Riders taking on Royal Challengers Bengaluru.

Two of the playoff matches will take place in Hyderabad while Eden Gardens will be the venue for the all-important second qualifier and the final on May 25.

It will be the first final at Eden Gardens in a decade, with the iconic stadium in Kolkata having previously hosted the 2013 and 2015 title showdowns.

IPL 2025 will have 74 matches spread over 65 days with 12 double-headers. Rajasthan Royals, Delhi Capitals, Lucknow Super Giants and Gujarat Titans will play three afternoon matches, which start at 3:30pm IST, each, while the other seven teams will play two day matches each.

Beyond the primary home bases of the 10 franchises in the IPL, the 2025 season will also spread its wings towards Guwahati (Royals' second home venue), Visakhapatnam (Capitals' second home venue) and Dharamsala (Punjab's second home venue). Guwahati and Vizag will host two matches each, Dharamsala will be the venue for three matches.

Ever since the IPL expanded to 10 franchises, the teams have been split into two groups. One features KKR, RCB, RR, CSK and PBKS while the other has SRH, DC, GT, LSG and MI. Franchises will meet the other teams in their group and one team in the other group twice, and the other four teams in the opposite group once.

1. Where can I watch IPL 2025 live matches?
Apart from attending the matches at the stadiums, fans can watch IPL 2025 live through the following options:
- TV: JioStar Network
- Online Streaming: JioHotstar app and website

2. When will the IPL 2025 start?
The 2025 edition of the Indian Premier League (IPL) will begin on Saturday, March 22.

3. How many teams will play in IPL 2025?
A total of 10 teams will participate in IPL 2025.

4. What is the venue list for IPL 2025 matches?
IPL 2025 will be held at 13 venues across India:
- Chennai: MA Chidambaram Stadium
- Ahmedabad: Narendra Modi Stadium
- Mumbai: Wankhede Stadium
- Bangalore: M. Chinnaswamy Stadium
- Delhi: Arun Jaitley Stadium
- Jaipur: Sawai Mansingh Stadium
- Hyderabad: Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium
- Kolkata: Eden Gardens
- Lucknow: Ekana Cricket Stadium
- Mullanpur: Maharaja Yadavindra Singh International Cricket Stadium
- Visakhapatnam: Dr. Y.S. Rajasekhara Reddy ACA-VDCA Cricket Stadium
- Dharamsala: HPCA Stadium
- Guwahati: Barsapara Stadium

5. Will there be any new rules in IPL 2025?
Yes, the BCCI has introduced an expanded player replacement policy for IPL 2025. Teams can now bring in substitutes for players suffering from season-ending injuries or illnesses. The replacement window has also been extended to the 12th league fixture, compared to the previous limit of the seventh match.
